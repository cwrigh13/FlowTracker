Coordinating Repairs Across Locations

The Challenge:

A library system with 8 branches shares a floating collection. When items break at one branch, they often need repair at another. Tracking cross-location transfers and repair status is chaos without a centralised system.

How FlowTracker Helps:

  • All branches see the same real-time dashboardâ€”no more duplicate work or lost items
  • Branch-specific collections with permission controls for sensitive items
  • Location transfers tracked with timestamps and audit trail

Academic Library

Tracking Special Collections Condition

The Challenge:

A university archives manages rare books, manuscripts, and historical artifacts requiring ongoing conservation. Paper-based condition reports are filed away and never reviewed. No one knows the full scope of preservation needs.

How FlowTracker Helps:

  • Digital condition reports with before/after photos documenting conservation work
  • Prioritise conservation work by condition severity and circulation demand
  • Track environmental monitoring issues (humidity, light exposure, pest damage)
  • Generate reports for grant applications and accreditation reviews

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FlowTracker

How long does implementation take?

Most libraries are fully operational within 30 minutes to 2 hours. Our guided onboarding wizard walks you through setup step-by-step. For larger Enterprise implementations with custom ILS integrations, full deployment typically takes 1-2 weeks including training.

What's included in the free trial?

The 30-day free trial includes full access to all features of the Professional tierâ€”no credit card required. You'll get unlimited issues, ILS integration, mobile access, notifications, reporting, and priority support during your trial. At the end, you can choose the tier that best fits your needs.

Can we import existing data from spreadsheets?

Yes! We provide CSV import tools and can help you migrate data from Excel, Google Sheets, or other systems. Professional and Enterprise customers get assisted migration support. We'll help you map your existing data structure to FlowTracker's fields.

How does ILS integration work?

FlowTracker connects to your ILS via API or web services. When you scan a barcode or enter an item number, we automatically fetch details like title, author, call number, and customer information from your ILS. We support Koha, Evergreen, Sierra, and can build custom adapters for other systems. Setup typically takes 15-30 minutes with your ILS credentials.

What browsers and devices are supported?

FlowTracker works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - current and previous versions). It's fully responsive and optimised for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices (iOS and Android). No software installation neededâ€”just a web browser and internet connection.

Where is our data stored?

All data is stored in secure data centres in Australia, ensuring compliance with Australian Privacy Act requirements. We use enterprise-grade encryption (TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest), daily backups, and complete multi-tenant isolation. Enterprise customers can request dedicated hosting or specific geographic requirements.

Can we change tiers later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade at any time with immediate access to new features and pro-rated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Essential and Professional tiers have no long-term contractsâ€”cancel anytime.

Do you offer training?

Yes! All tiers include access to our video tutorial library, help centre, and written guides. Professional tier includes live training webinars. Enterprise customers get personalised onboarding sessions, custom training materials, and ongoing education workshops.

What if we need custom features?

Enterprise customers can request custom development, ILS adapters, or workflow modifications. We also maintain a public feature roadmap where all customers can vote on upcoming features. Popular requests are prioritised in our development schedule.
